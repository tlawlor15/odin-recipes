Lasagna

Description

This is how to make lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sausage

Steps

  1. Grab your ingredients
  2. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  3. Layer the pasta, tomato sauce, cheese and sausage on top of one another
  4. Place in the oven
  5. Wait for a few minutes
  6. Enjoy!
